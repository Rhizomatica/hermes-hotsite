WHY WE BUILT HERMES?
Together with social organizations and remote communities, first in the Amazon rainforest, and then around the world, we have been dreaming about, designing, building and testing HERMES since 2017 to create reliable, secure, long-range, autonomous voice and digital communications.
LONG-DISTANCE COMMUNICATIONS
Powered by HF radio, HERMES enables voice and data communication over vast distances (hundreds of kilometers) with very low power consumption and without the need for Internet or satellites. It can be installed practically anywhere.
SECURE COMMUNICATIONS
  • As an option, HERMES uses state of the art encryption and password protection to keep communications safe from prying eyes and ears.
  • HERMES is compatible with HAM radio regulations as all security features are optional and software-configurable.
REMOTE MONITORING
Due to its low power usage and small size, HERMES can be installed in both mobile and fixed remote monitoring settings, such as boats or weather stations, to send real-time GPS coordinates, photos and sensor data.

WHAT'S IN THE BOX?
HARDWARE
  • Software Defined Radio (SDR) with 25 Watts of output power on 80 M to 10 M bands
  • SSB digital voice radio with PTT microphone
  • Compute and interface via RPi 5 with a 7-inch touch screen
  • Powered with 12VDC. Power consumption in receive: 600 mA, transmit: 9A
  • Measures 25cm x 15cm x 5cm and weights about 2Kgs. About the size of a hardcover book.
