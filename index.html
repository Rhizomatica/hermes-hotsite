WHY WE BUILT HERMES?
Together with social organizations and remote communities in the Amazon rainforest, we have been dreaming about, designing, testing and building HERMES since 2017 to create reliable, secure, long- range, autonomous voice and digital communications.
LONG-DISTANCE COMMUNICATIONS
Powered by HF radio, HERMES enables voice and data communication over vast distances (hundreds of kilometers) with very low power consumption and without the need for Internet or satellites. It can be installed practically anywhere.
SECURE COMMUNICATIONS
As an option, HERMES uses state of the art encryption and password protection to keep communications safe from prying eyes and ears. It is also compatible with HAM radio regulations as all security features are optional and software-configurable.
REMOTE MONITORING
Due to its low power usage and small size, HERMES can be installed in both mobile and fixed remote monitoring settings, such as boats or weather stations, to send real-time GPS coordinates, photos and whatever other information is needed.
